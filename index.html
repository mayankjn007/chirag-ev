About Us

We are the official dealership for Panther EV in Maharashtra, bringing you durable, cost-effective electric vehicles with exceptional after-sales support.

Chirag EV is a trusted supplier of high-quality and durable electric vehicles, including E-Rickshaws, E-Loaders, and E-Scooters. Our products are manufactured in a state-of-the-art facility that adheres to international best practices in manufacturing, safety, and environmental responsibility.

With a monthly production capacity of 1,500 vehicles, our team of experienced engineers and technicians is committed to ensuring complete customer satisfaction. All Panther EV products are approved by ICAT, Manesar, and undergo rigorous quality checks and calibration to meet the latest industry standards.

The rapid rise of Panther as a reliable brand in the EV space is a testament to its quality, performance, and trust built over time.
